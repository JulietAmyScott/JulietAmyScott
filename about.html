me & design

design bio

I believe it is important to tap into cultural and social movements - not as a vehicle to exploit or ride a short-term trend - but to create a message in line with a vision of what the future might look like, and how to improve it. My interests include inclusivity, sustainability, and using new methods to connect with consumers in the present and tomorrow. I want to establish a subtle sense of the product beyond its accepted function to create a visceral sense of well-being and confidence in the brand. My goal - through design - is to inspire conversation among consumers and convert them to enthusiasts, or strengthen an established relationship.

free time

I have been active in animal welfare for many years. For the last four years, I have volunteered with LA Animal Services. During that time, I implemented ideas to educate the public about the shelter's small animals, which helped increase adoption rates. I have designed promotional flyers for adoption events and maintain a social media account, smalls of san pedro, focused on shelter small animal adoption and care information.
